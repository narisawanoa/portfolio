Plot for the plotter

September 2025 / Multimedia Installation
I made a walking track for the plotter, generating a walking path in real time. I added several variables to track the walk, such as distance in pixels, duration time in seconds, motor usage, and the density of lines. I used white pencil on white paper to focus viewers on the internal algorithms of the plotter for it to walk.

In communication with an unknown Entity

May 2025 / Multimedia Installation
An interactive software where the viewer could reflect responses to carry out conversation with the forest. I translated the software into physical installation by making an object that can receive signals from the classroom and responds to them.

No more understanding of the world

April 2025 / Multimedia Installation
A live projection of the sky recording on site. The sky is divided by invisible borders that can easily be trespassed. Yet, I am bounded on the land unable to see the other skies but my own.

Mission Statement

March 2025 / Multimedia Installation
A site-specific installation for RISD beach. The mission statement of RISD administration is played on repeat from a speaker inside an enclosed cube, filtered by the cube and the traffic noise surrounding the institution.
