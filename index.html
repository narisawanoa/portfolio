Plot for the plotter

I made a walking track for the plotter. I made the plotter walk, generating a walking path for the plotter in real time. From the initial script I wrote in Processing, I asked claude to translate from Java to Python. I added several variables to track the walk, such as distance in pixels, duration time in seconds, motor usage, the density of lines, etc. The walk will be executed in rounds while each rounds will have diifferent tendencies in randomness that I have applied to the walk. Such variables are visualized in real time. I used white pencil on white paper, which makes us difficult to see the traces of walk by the plotter. I made this choice in order for the viewers to focus on the internal argorithms of the plotter for it to walk. I stayed away from humanizing the plotter to highlight the plotter's inner complexity without emotion or consciousness.

In communication with an unknown Entity

May 2025 / Multimedia Installation (plexi glass, mirror panel, arduino, processing)

This is a project that derived from an interactive software I made, in which the viewer could relect responses to carry out conversatioin with the forest. I then translated the software into physical installation with softwares by making an object that can receive signals from the classroom and responds to them. The objects were places in the distant forest, far from the classroom. The box in the classroom is a replica of the object placed in the classroom.

No more understanding of the world (Live recording and projection of the sky)

April 2025 / Multimedia Installation

This is a live projection of the sky that is recording live on site. The sky is something that is devided by invisible borders that can easily be trespassed. Yet, I am bounded on the land unable to see the other skys but my own as I have no means to escape my own sky, that is, political, economical, and personal situations that bound me to this place.

Mission Statement

March 2025 / Multimedia Installation

This is a site specific installation I made for RISD beach. The mission statement of RISD administratioin is played on repeat from a speaker inside an enclosed cube. RISD beach was chosen as a site that symbolizes the institution where students gather. The mission statement played from the speaker is flitered by the cube that represents the institution and the traffic noise surrounding the institution.
